Boarding Options

Going on vacation? We have many options for boarding your hose that are so comfortable, they won't want to come home. Seriously, your horse will not want to go back to its crappy barn in your backyard. These boarding options are like mansions for horses, so get ready to be amazed.

Barn Stalls
(with in and out)

12' x12' box stall w/ optional access to 12' x 24' outside pen with shelter, bedding with 2 bags of shavings oer week (extra shavings available for purchase).

Large Barn
(Mare Motel)

Extra large 24'x48' corral fully covered center aisle with two full shelters and seperate feeders! Twice daily feeding of high quality hay.

Pasture

Beautiful open scenery, shade trees and/or large shelter depending upon pasture, large hill for climbing fun! Available for multiple or singles, if you would like your horse to socialize!
