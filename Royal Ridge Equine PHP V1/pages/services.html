Our Services

The Royal Ridge and Serenity Equestrian Center strives to be the center of your equestrian life. We have various boarding options in sizes and amenities to cover temporary requirements or more permanent needs. We also offer an array of lessons and training to educate the novice rider to more demanding training for our more experienced clients.
