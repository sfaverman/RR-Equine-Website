About the Royal Ridge Equestrian Center

At Royal Ridge and Serenity Equestrian Centers, we always envisioned building a horse facility that provides all the amenities desired by every horse person. We are committed to keeping the horses and riders happy. The manager, owner and head trainer, Emily Richards, strives to meet every need of the horse and rider.

This facility was designed to provide a safe, comfortable and accessible environment for you and your horse. The property is gated with an on site manager.

There are over 100 stalls, several riding areans (multiple lighted arenas), round pens and numerous trails. We offer a wide variety of stabling options.

Riding arenas are kept well groomed to provide a rewarding experience. The trails on site encircle the property and have a variety of terrains, providing a great place to warm-up, cool-down, gallop, condition, or just relax.

The Arenas have sprinklers. Barns offer large breezeways and stalls are cleaned daily. Horses are fed high quality bermuda or alfalfa hay.

Always striving for the best, we are in a never-ending pursuit of being the best facility in San Diego.
